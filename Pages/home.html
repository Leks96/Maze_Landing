About

The Maze Project is an innovative initiative that leverages the power of raycasting and SDL2 to create an immersive 3D gaming experience. Our goal is to provide a fun and educational platform where users can explore the intricacies of 3D graphics.

Features

  • Advanced Raycasting Engine: Experience smooth and accurate 3D rendering.

  • Intuitive Controls: Navigate with ease using keyboard and mouse inputs.

  • Dynamic Textures: Enjoy rich and detailed environments with customizable textures.

  • Collision Detection: Realistic interactions and obstacle avoidance.

  • Expandable Maps: Easily add and modify map files for endless exploration.
